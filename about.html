Our Background

A Movement Born of God

Eternal Excellencies Women Ministry (EEWM) was birthed from a deep conviction that women are not an afterthought in God's redemptive plan — they are central to it. Founded under the visionary leadership of Dr. Jane Mwikali Makau, EEWM emerged as a bold response to the call of Isaiah 60:15.

Rooted in Kenya and housed at Kingdom Envoys Ministries, this interdenominational movement gathers women from all denominations on every second Saturday of the month — not to build a church, but to build women who will build the church, the family, the marketplace, and the nation.

EEWM is neither an NGO nor a conventional church group. It stands at the intersection of the spiritual and the developmental — a space where women encounter God deeply and are equipped to thrive powerfully in every area of life.

👁

Our Vision

To see every woman walk in the fullness of her God-given identity, purpose, and potential — spiritually grounded, personally empowered, and generationally impactful.

Our Mission

To disciple, mentor, and empower women through prayer, the Word, fellowship, and practical skills — raising a generation of women who are a joy to their families, communities, and nations.

Our Core Values

01

Interdenominational Unity

We welcome all women regardless of denomination. Our unity is in Christ, not in tradition.

02

Spiritual Depth

We go beyond surface-level Christianity into deep discipleship, prayer, and prophetic purpose.

Holistic Empowerment

Generational Legacy

President & Founder

Dr. Jane Mwikali Makau

Reverend & Ordained Minister Chair, Kenya Betting Control & Licensing Board Author & Public Speaker Women Empowerment Advocate

Dr. Jane Mwikali Makau is a woman marked by divine purpose. An ordained minister, a nationally recognised public servant, and a passionate advocate for women, she carries a rare anointing that bridges the sacred and the secular — the pulpit and the boardroom.

As the visionary founder of EEWM, Dr. Jane has created a movement that reflects her own journey: one of deep faith, relentless excellence, and a burning desire to see women rise into the fullness of who God created them to be. Her teaching is both prophetically sharp and practically empowering, drawing women into encounters with God that transform their lives from the inside out.

"God has not forsaken you. He is making you majestic — and your life will become a joy to generations you have not yet met." — Dr. Jane Mwikali Makau
